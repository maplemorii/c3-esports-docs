Welcome to C3 Esports. This section covers everything you need as a **player** — from joining a team to checking in to matches and understanding your stats.

<Note>
  Players are different from team managers. As a player, you can check in to matches and view your schedule, but only your team manager can submit scores, file disputes, or make roster changes. If you need something changed that requires manager action, reach out to your manager directly.
</Note>

## What players do on the platform

## Account requirements to compete

Before you can join a team and play matches, you must complete these steps on your C3 Esports account:

| Requirement | Guide |
|---|---|
| Create an account | [Creating Your Account](/getting-started/creating-your-account) |
| Verify your .edu email | [Creating Your Account](/getting-started/creating-your-account) |
| Link your Discord account | [Linking Discord](/getting-started/linking-discord) |

<Warning>
  You will not appear in roster searches or be able to use team invite codes until both your .edu email is verified **and** your Discord account is linked. Complete both steps before asking your manager to add you.
</Warning>

## Player vs. manager permissions

| Action | Player | Manager |
|---|---|---|
| Check in to a match | Yes | Yes |
| View match schedule | Yes | Yes |
| Upload replay files | Yes | Yes |
| View standings | Yes | Yes |
| Submit match results | No | Yes |
| File a dispute | No | Yes |
| Add/remove roster players | No | Yes |
| Register for a season | No | Yes |
| Edit team settings | No | Yes |

## Getting help

- For platform questions, visit **#player-support** in the [C3 Esports Discord](https://discord.gg/c3esports).
- For match-specific issues, contact your team manager first — they have the tools to act on your behalf.
- For rule questions, check the [Rules](/rules/eligibility) section of these docs.
