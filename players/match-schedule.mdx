Your match schedule shows all upcoming and past matches for your team in the current season. Matches are scheduled by staff at the start of each match week — you do not choose your match time.

<Note>
  All match times on the platform are displayed in **Eastern Time (ET)**. If you are in a different time zone, convert accordingly. Check before match day, not five minutes before check-in.
</Note>

## Reading the schedule

Each match entry shows:

| Field | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Teams** | Your team vs. the opposing team |
| **Date and time** | Scheduled match start in Eastern Time |
| **Division** | Which division this match counts toward |
| **Week** | Which regular season week this is (e.g., Week 3) |
| **Format** | BO3 (regular season) or BO5 (playoffs, typically) |
| **Status** | Scheduled, In Progress, Completed, Forfeited, or Cancelled |

## Regular season vs. playoff schedule

- **Regular season weeks** are scheduled by staff at the start of the season. Each team typically plays one match per week.
- **Playoffs** are single-elimination (or another format announced by staff) and are scheduled after regular season standings are finalized. Playoff schedules are announced in Discord and on the Seasons page.

<Tip>
  Mark your match dates on a personal calendar as soon as the schedule is published. Missing check-in is a forfeit, and "I forgot" is not a valid reason for a reschedule.
</Tip>

## Rescheduling a match

Rescheduling must be agreed upon by **both** managers and approved by staff. To request a reschedule:

1. Contact the opposing manager in Discord and agree on a new time.
2. Both managers message **#reschedule-requests** in the C3 Esports Discord with the original match time and the agreed new time.
3. Staff confirm or deny the reschedule.

<Warning>
  Do not assume a reschedule is approved until staff confirm it. Matches that are not officially rescheduled will be treated as occurring at the original time — if your team does not check in, it is a forfeit.
</Warning>

## Time zone reference

| Your time zone | ET offset (EST) | ET offset (EDT) |
|---|---|---|
| Pacific (PT) | +3 hours | +3 hours |
| Mountain (MT) | +2 hours | +2 hours |
| Central (CT) | +1 hour | +1 hour |
| Eastern (ET) | — | — |

EST (Eastern Standard Time) is UTC-5. EDT (Eastern Daylight Time, summer) is UTC-4.

<Tip>
  Use a site like [timeanddate.com](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html) to convert match times to your local time zone quickly.
</Tip>
