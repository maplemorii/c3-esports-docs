The C3 Esports staff panel is accessible to users with the **STAFF** role (or the **ADMIN** role, which includes all staff access). Staff are the league's operations team — they manage seasons, review registrations, resolve disputes, and keep match schedules running smoothly.

## Who has access

| Role | Staff panel access |
|---|---|
| `USER` | No |
| `STAFF` | Yes — all sections listed below |
| `ADMIN` | Yes — plus user management, audit log, announcements, and cron jobs |

Role assignments are managed by admins. If you need staff access, contact an admin via the C3 Esports Discord.

## Staff responsibilities by season phase

| Season phase | Key staff tasks |
|---|---|
| **Off-season** | Set up the next season, configure dates and divisions |
| **Registration** | Review and approve team registrations daily |
| **Active (regular season)** | Monitor matches weekly, resolve disputes, handle no-shows |
| **Playoffs** | Schedule bracket matches, resolve disputes quickly, monitor for sportsmanship issues |
| **Completed** | Finalize standings, archive the season, announce results |

---

## Staff communication

Staff coordinate in the **#staff** channel in the C3 Esports Discord. Major decisions — dispute rulings, registration denials, rule interpretations — should be discussed there before taking action, especially for edge cases.

<Tip>
  When in doubt, ask before acting. A second opinion from a fellow staff member is always worth the few minutes it takes, especially for anything that affects a team's standing or ability to compete.
</Tip>

## Getting started as a new staff member

If you have just been granted staff access:

1. Read the [Season Management](/staff/season-management) guide to understand the season structure.
2. Read the [Dispute Resolution](/staff/dispute-resolution) guide — disputes are the most time-sensitive responsibility.
3. Familiarize yourself with the [Match Rules](/rules/match-rules), [Eligibility Rules](/rules/eligibility), and [Dispute Policy](/rules/dispute-policy) so you can make informed decisions.
4. Introduce yourself in **#staff** on Discord and ask an existing staff member to walk you through any active tasks.
