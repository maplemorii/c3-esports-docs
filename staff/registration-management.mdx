title: "Registration Management"

---

When registration is open, team managers submit their teams for the upcoming season. Your job as staff is to review each registration, verify eligibility, and approve or deny with a clear reason.

---

## Accessing the registration queue

Go to [c3esports.com/admin/registrations](https://c3esports.com/admin/registrations). The queue shows all registrations with status `PENDING` for the current (or selected) season, sorted by submission time.

## Reviewing a registration

Click on any pending registration to open the detail view. This shows:

- **Team info**: name, tag, logo, description
- **Division requested**: Premier, Challengers, or Contenders
- **Manager**: who submitted and when
- **Roster**: all players on the roster at time of submission, with eligibility indicators

![Registration detail view showing team info at top, roster table with eligibility checkmarks, and Approve/Deny buttons at the bottom](../images/registration-management-detail.png)

### Eligibility indicators

Each player row shows three eligibility flags:

| Flag | Meaning |
|---|---|
| .edu verified | Player has a verified .edu email |
| Discord linked | Player has connected a Discord account |
| No duplicate roster | Player is not on another active team |

<Warning>
  A registration can be submitted with ineligible players. The platform does not block submission — it only displays warnings. You must review these manually. A team that goes into the season with ineligible players will cause disputes and headaches down the road.
</Warning>

---

## Approving a registration

If the roster has at least 3 eligible players and there are no red flags, click **Approve**. The team's status changes from `PENDING` to `REGISTERED` and they are added to the standings for their division (with 0 points).

The manager receives a notification on the platform and a Discord notification if they have that enabled.

<Tip>
  If a registration has 1–2 ineligible players but still meets the 3-player minimum with eligible players, you can approve with a warning note. Include in your approval notes that the flagged players must resolve eligibility before competing.
</Tip>

---

## Denying a registration

If a registration cannot be approved as-is, click **Deny** and provide a clear, actionable reason.

### Good denial reasons (specific and fixable):
- "Roster has only 2 eligible players — 3 minimum required. PlayerX needs to verify their .edu email."
- "Team tag 'UTA' is already taken by another registered team. Please choose a different tag."
- "PlayerY appears on two rosters simultaneously. Remove them from the other team before resubmitting."

### Avoid vague denial reasons:
- "Ineligible" (which requirement? which player?)
- "Please fix and resubmit" (fix what?)

<Note>
  After denial, the team's status returns to `DRAFT` and the manager can resubmit during the registration window. Make sure your denial reason gives them everything they need to fix the issue.
</Note>

## Bulk approving registrations

If you have many clean registrations (all green eligibility flags, correct roster sizes, unique names/tags), you can bulk-approve:

1. On the registrations list, check the checkboxes next to all registrations you want to approve.
2. Click **Bulk Approve** in the action bar that appears.
3. Confirm. All selected registrations are approved simultaneously.

<Warning>
  Review each registration before bulk-approving. The bulk action does not show individual eligibility warnings — you must have already reviewed each one individually. Do not use bulk approve as a shortcut to skip review.
</Warning>

## Division assignment review

Pay attention to the division each team is requesting. If a team's roster is clearly overqualified or underqualified for their chosen division, note it:

- You may approve but add a note flagging the mismatch
- You may contact the manager via Discord to discuss a better division fit
- Staff cannot force a division change, but you can deny and ask them to resubmit in a more appropriate division if there is a clear case

See [Division Tiers](/reference/division-tiers) for skill level descriptions per division.

---

## After registration closes

Once the registration window closes:

1. Make sure all pending registrations have been acted on (approved or denied). No applications should remain in `PENDING` status.
2. If a registration was missed and the window has closed, contact an admin — admins can re-open the window briefly or manually update the status.
3. Proceed to [Season Management](/staff/season-management) to schedule match weeks.
