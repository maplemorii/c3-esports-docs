Announcements are platform-wide messages displayed to all users when they log in or visit the dashboard. They are ideal for communicating season dates, rule changes, maintenance windows, and other important information.

<Note>
  Announcements are visible to all users regardless of their team, division, or role. Use them for information that is genuinely relevant to the whole platform — not for messages that only affect one team or one match.
</Note>

---

## Creating an announcement

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Announcements page">
    Navigate to [c3esports.com/admin/announcements](https://c3esports.com/admin/announcements).

    {/* TODO: add screenshot */}
    ![Admin announcements page showing a list of past announcements and a Create Announcement button](../images/announcements-page.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Announcement">
    Click the **Create Announcement** button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the announcement details">
    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | **Title** | Short, descriptive headline (required) |
    | **Body** | Full announcement text. Markdown is supported for formatting. |
    | **Type** | Info, Warning, or Critical — controls the visual style (blue, yellow, red banner) |
    | **Publish date** | When the announcement becomes visible. Leave blank to publish immediately. |
    | **Expiry date** | When the announcement automatically stops showing. Optional. |

    {/* TODO: add screenshot */}
    ![Announcement creation form showing title, body, type selector, and date picker fields](../images/announcements-create-form.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and publish">
    Click **Preview** to see how the announcement will look to users, then click **Publish**. The announcement is live immediately (or at the scheduled publish date).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Announcement types

Choose the type that matches the urgency of your message:

| Type | Color | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| **Info** | Blue | General news, schedule updates, new features |
| **Warning** | Yellow | Upcoming deadlines, registration closing soon, policy reminders |
| **Critical** | Red | Server maintenance, urgent rule clarifications, emergency changes |

<Tip>
  Reserve Critical announcements for genuine emergencies. Over-using the red banner trains users to ignore it.
</Tip>

## Managing existing announcements

From the announcements list, you can:

- **Edit** any announcement (click the pencil icon). Changes take effect immediately.
- **Unpublish** an announcement to hide it without deleting it.
- **Delete** an announcement permanently. Deleted announcements do not appear in the audit log body, but the deletion action itself is logged.

## Announcement best practices

- **Be clear and concise.** Users skim announcements. Put the most important information in the first sentence.
- **Include a deadline if one exists.** "Registration closes Friday at 11:59 PM ET" is better than "registration is closing soon."
- **Set an expiry date.** Outdated announcements create confusion. Set an expiry for anything time-sensitive.
- **Use the Warning type for deadlines.** Yellow draws attention without the alarm of red.
- **Cross-post to Discord.** Announcements are on the platform, but most users live in Discord. Always post important information in the relevant Discord channel as well.
