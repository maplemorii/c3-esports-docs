## Current playoff process (manual)

Until the bracket system is available, staff manage playoff scheduling manually:

1. At the end of the regular season, staff pull the final standings from each division.
2. Staff create playoff match records manually via the [Match Management](/staff/match-management) panel.
3. Bracket pairings follow standard seeding: seed 1 vs. seed 8, seed 2 vs. seed 7, etc. (or the number of teams that qualify from each division).
4. Winners advance and staff create the next round's matches.
5. Standings and results are tracked the same way as regular season matches.

<Note>
  If you are a team in playoffs right now, you do not need to do anything differently. The playoff match will appear in your match schedule and the check-in, result submission, and replay upload process is identical to the regular season.
</Note>

When bracket management is implemented, it will include:

  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-advancement" icon="arrow-right">
    When a playoff match result is confirmed, the winner automatically advances to the next round's match slot.
  </Card>
