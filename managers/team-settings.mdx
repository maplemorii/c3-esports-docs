The Team Settings page is where you manage everything about your team's identity and leadership. Only the team manager can access these settings.

To get there: open your team page and click the **Settings** tab (or the gear icon in the team header).

## Editing team information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Navigate to your team page and click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the fields you want to change">
    You can edit any combination of the following:

    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | **Team name** | Must be unique, 3–50 characters |
    | **Team tag** | Must be unique, 2–5 characters |
    | **Logo** | Square PNG or JPG, min 256×256px |
    | **Description** | Free text, displayed on your public team page |

    <Note>
      Team name and tag changes are subject to review. If your new name violates the [Code of Conduct](/rules/code-of-conduct), staff will revert it.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save Changes**. Updates take effect immediately on your team page and in standings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Regenerating the invite code

If your team's invite code has been shared with the wrong people, invalidate it immediately:

1. In Team Settings, scroll to the **Invite Code** section.
2. Click **Regenerate Invite Code**.
3. Confirm in the dialog. The old code stops working instantly.
4. Share the new code only with trusted players.

<Warning>
  Regenerating the invite code does **not** remove anyone already on the roster. If someone joined who should not have, remove them manually from the Roster tab before regenerating.
</Warning>

## Transferring the manager role

You can hand off the manager role to any current member of your roster.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings and find the Danger Zone">
    Scroll to the bottom of the settings page. The **Manager Transfer** option is in this section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a new manager">
    Choose a player from your roster in the dropdown. Only current roster members are eligible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transfer">
    Click **Transfer Manager Role** and confirm. The transfer is immediate.

    <Warning>
      This action is **irreversible** without the new manager's cooperation. Once transferred, you become a regular player. The new manager must transfer the role back to you if you want it again.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Before transferring the role, make sure the player you are transferring to understands their responsibilities — result submission, roster management, dispute filing. Share the [Managers Overview](/managers/overview) page with them.
</Tip>

## Disbanding the team

Disbanding permanently deletes the team. This cannot be undone.

<Warning>
  Do not disband your team during an active season. Doing so forfeits all remaining scheduled matches and removes your team from standings. Contact staff first if you need to withdraw mid-season.
</Warning>

To disband:

1. Go to **Team Settings → Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Disband Team**.
3. Type your team name to confirm, then click **Confirm Disband**.

All roster members are removed from the team. Their accounts remain active and they can join or create other teams.

## What you cannot change

Some settings are locked once a season registration is approved:

- **Division** — requires staff approval to change mid-season
- **Team tag** — may be locked if matches are already scheduled (contact staff)

For anything that appears greyed out or locked, open a support ticket in the [C3 Esports Discord](https://discord.gg/c3esports).
